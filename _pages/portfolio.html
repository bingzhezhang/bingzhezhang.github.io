--- layout: archive title: "News" permalink: /portfolio/ author_profile: true --- Dr. Bingzhe Zhang Successfully Defends Doctoral Dissertation! ------ Bingzhe Zhang successfully defended his doctoral dissertation titled Study on Seismic Performance and Influencing Factors of Laminated Rubber Bearing Supported Bridges based on Deep Neural Networks at Southeast University, Nanjing.
